Virginia Dare

We’re on hiatus because we live on opposite coasts.

The name Virginia Dare has a lot of baggage and history. It's unfortunately been used for a group white supremacists, may they die or come back to reality instead of dragging us back to nazi times!

It was also the name of a popular brand of wine, mentioned in the poem “Harlem Sweeties” by Langston Hughes. Here's an excerpt:

        Glow of the quince
        To blush of the rose. 
        Persimmon bronze
        To cinnamon toes.
        Blackberry cordial,
        Virginia Dare wine—
        All those sweet colors
        Flavor Harlem of mine!
